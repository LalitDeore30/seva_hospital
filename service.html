Our Services

1. Our well-equipped laboratories perform a wide range of tests, including blood tests, urine tests, genetic testing, and more.

2. We have advanced imaging technologies, including X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, ultrasound, and mammography, to aid in the diagnosis of diseases and injuries.

3. Our pathology department specializes in examining tissues, fluids, and cells to identify diseases and help guide treatment decisions.

4. We offer a broad range of general surgical procedures, such as appendectomy, hernia repair, gallbladder removal, and more.

5. Our orthopedic surgeons specialize in procedures related to bones.

6. Accommodation and meals at the standard ward care .

7. Nursing services, when provided by the hospital .

8. Laboratory, X-ray and diagnostic procedures, and interpretation .

9. Drugs prescribed by a physician and administered in the hospital .

10. Radiotherapy treatment, occupational therapy, and physiotherapy when provided by an insured .
